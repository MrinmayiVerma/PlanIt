Non-governmental organization (NGO)

A Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) is a non-profit,
voluntary group that operates independently of the government to address
social, environmental, humanitarian, or developmental issues.
NGOs work towards the welfare of communities by providing
support, advocacy, and resources in areas such as
education, healthcare, human rights, and disaster relief.

Pratham Education Foundation

Pratham works to improve basic literacy and numeracy in rural areas. They run large-scale programs like "Read India" to help children develop essential skills. Donations support education and community development programs.
